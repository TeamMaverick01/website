Our Technical Departments

Chassis

Engineering the structural backbone of the vehicle through advanced skeletal design. We prioritize maximizing torsional rigidity and optimizing ergonomic integration to ensure unparalleled safety and a seamless driver-machine interface.

Vehicle Dynamics

The mastery of motion. We leverage complex kinematic simulations to refine suspension geometry and tire contact patch behavior, ensuring razor-sharp handling and superior mechanical grip in every corner.

Aerodynamics

Sculpting airflow for competitive advantage. Utilizing high-fidelity Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), we manipulate boundary layers to generate aggressive downforce while meticulously neutralizing parasitic drag.

Braking

Precision-engineered deceleration. Our team designs bespoke rotor profiles and high-performance assemblies to achieve consistent, fade-resistant stopping power and optimized thermal dissipation.

Transmission

The conduit of power. We develop custom drivetrain architectures and high-speed shifting mechanisms engineered for instantaneous torque transfer and maximum driveline efficiency.

Powertrain / LV

Orchestrating the heart and nervous system of the vehicle. We focus on extracting peak thermodynamic performance from the engine while managing a robust Low Voltage (LV) architecture for mission-critical data and control.

Sponsorship and PR

Cultivating the strategic capital and corporate alliances that fuel our engineering breakthroughs. We bridge the gap between technical excellence and industry-leading brands through high-value value propositions.

Media Team

Translating complex engineering into a compelling narrative. Through high-impact visual storytelling and cinematic production, we capture the technical essence and competitive spirit of the team for a global audience.
